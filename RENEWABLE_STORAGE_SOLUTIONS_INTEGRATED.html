🔴 Real-Time Impact Validator

These problems aren't historical—they're happening right now. StorageScout (companion platform) tracks live solar generation across the top 10 US energy storage states, measuring real-time curtailment (wasted clean energy) every 60 seconds.

How it works:
  • Open-Meteo API feeds real-time solar radiation (W/m²) for 10 major cities
  • System calculates opportunity score: (current_radiation / baseline) × (historical_curtailment / capacity) × (market_demand weight)
  • High opportunity = high solar + high curtailment history + insufficient storage = PROOF the problem is real RIGHT NOW
What this means for solutions:
  • StorageIQ Dashboard: Real-time data shows exactly when batteries should dispatch to capture grid peak prices. Value: visible in live pricing moments.
  • GridMatch AI: High-opportunity windows (peak solar + curtailment risk) are exact moments when storage can earn grid revenue. Platform identifies these hourly.
  • BatteryLife Extended: Cycle stress is measurable in real-time. During high-curtailment periods, battery stress increases 40-60%. Monitoring solution prevents degradation in these exact moments.
  • HybridFlow Optimizer: Daily solar curves show why different battery types are needed—some catch morning peak (short duration), others hold for evening load (long duration). Mix optimization is quantifiable hourly.
