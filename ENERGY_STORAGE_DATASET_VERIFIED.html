📚 Primary Sources & Verification

Deloitte 2026 Renewable Energy Industry Outlook - Published October 2025 by Deloitte Research Center for Energy & Industrials. Comprehensive analysis of 5 key trends affecting renewables: Policy shifts, Storage integration, Capital efficiency, M&A activity, Supply chain agility. Authors: Keith Adams, Kate Hardin, Shih Yu (Elsie) Hung. https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/insights/industry/renewable-energy/renewable-energy-industry-outlook.html
International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA) - Global renewable energy data, capacity tracking, cost reduction trends, and policy frameworks. IRENA maintains comprehensive databases on renewable energy capacity, generation, and employment. Primary data source for global trends. https://www.irena.org/Data
U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) - Official US government energy statistics. Provides data on electricity generation, storage capacity, costs, and state-level renewable portfolio standards. https://www.eia.gov/
FERC (Federal Energy Regulatory Commission) - US regulatory authority for electricity markets. Provides interconnection queue data (377 GW backlog cited from FERC data), market reforms (Order 755, Order 2222), and regional transmission operator policies.
National Conference of State Legislatures (NCSL) - Data on state renewable portfolio standards, policy changes (Ohio RPS sunset, North Carolina rollbacks), and regional regulatory variations.
World Economic Forum - Global Energy 2026 - Forecasts on energy storage growth, capital requirements, and global market trends through 2030. Published December 2025.
MarketsandMarkets Research - Commercial market intelligence firm providing global energy storage market size, revenue projections ($668.7B in 2024 → $5.12T by 2034), and regional trends.
IRS Tax Credit Guidance & FEOC Rules - Official tax code and sourcing restrictions affecting renewable energy economics. One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA) changes 2025. FEOC restrictions effective 2026.
