CORE PROBLEMS

1. FINANCIAL BARRIERS
   - Annual funding gap: $190 billion
   - Required investment by 2030: $600 billion
   - Current available: $410 billion
   - Battery costs remain high for widespread deployment
   - Tax incentives are changing, creating uncertainty
   - Investment activity declining (deal volume down 89% in early 2025)

2. REGULATORY & POLICY CHALLENGES
   - Interconnection backlog: 377 GW of approved projects waiting
   - Credit risk: 83% of planned storage may lose tax incentives under new rules
   - Policy instability: Rules changing, reducing investor confidence
   - Regional inconsistency: 10+ different regulatory frameworks across the US

3. TECHNICAL GAPS
   - Current technology: 2-4 hour battery storage
   - Required capability: 8-24+ hour storage systems
   - Efficiency loss: 5-15% per charge/discharge cycle
   - Deployment gap: Have 37.4 GW installed, need 187 GW by 2030
   - Emerging technology still developing (not yet production-ready)

PROPOSED SOLUTIONS

1. **StorageIQ Dashboard**
   - Problem addressed: Inefficient use of existing battery systems

   - Revenue timeline: 4-6 months to payback
   - Addressable market: 37.4 GW of operating installations

2. **GridMatch AI**
   - Problem addressed: Storage operators cannot access grid revenue opportunities
   - Implementation time: 6-12 months
   - Market opportunity: $18.7 billion addressable
   - Regulatory support: FERC Order 2222 creates accelerated adoption
   - Benefit: New revenue stream for storage operators

3. **BatteryLife Extended**
   - Problem addressed: Battery replacement costs drive project economics
   - Customer value: $2 million to $8 million per installation
   - Revenue model: Recurring monitoring and optimization
   - Addressable market: Global battery installations
   - Benefit: Extend asset lifespan, reduce replacement costs

4. **HybridFlow Optimizer**
   - Problem addressed: Inefficient mix of storage technologies
   - Cost benefit: 25-35% reduction in total storage costs
   - Addressable market: 187 GW pipeline of new projects
   - Market requirement: Emerging regulations favor technology diversity
   - Benefit: Lower costs for hybrid storage systems
